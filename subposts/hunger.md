## Hunger is the best spice

Food tastes better when you're hungry. Even the most delicious meal is painful to eat if you're full. You already know this. The interesting case is how it generalizes to time.

### Infinite hours are worth nothing per hour.

When I had unlimited free time, I got nothing done. Whole afternoons evaporated. The hours weren't valuable because they were infinite.

Then I went back to school and took on more contract work. I expected my hobbies to suffer.

The opposite happened. I started shipping. Two free hours after a working day produced more than two days of fucking around.

Quit your job to write the novel and the novel never gets written. There's too much food. Now you're not hungry enough to eat. Make the meal smaller. You'll taste it.
