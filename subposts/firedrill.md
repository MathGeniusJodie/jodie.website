## Fire Drill

It's easy to not notice the impact a habit has, because it becomes your new normal. People go back to bad habits in moments of vulnerability. If you feel like shit, and you do the habit that makes you feel like shit, you won't feel the difference. You'll go back to the old habits without noticing it's hurting you.

So schedule a deliberate day of self destruction. Go back to all your bad habits.

Bad habits aren't addictions. The habit's badness is the whole experience. There's no hit to hook you back in. Stay up till 4am! Skip the gym! Have terrible posture! The crucial part is that you need to do this when you are mentally healthy and feeling great.

The reasons won't be theoretical anymore. You'll know, from your lizard brain to your superego, that you *actually do not want to*.
