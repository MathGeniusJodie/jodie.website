## The Emotional Cyborg

<p>One limitation of therapy is that you can't have a therapist be with you 24/7 to examine your behavior. Luckily we now have <abbr>ai</abbr> that can monitor everything you say, and correct bad thought patterns and behaviours in real time.
Computers turned us into information and math cyborgs, now they allow us to enhance our emotional intelligence.</p>
