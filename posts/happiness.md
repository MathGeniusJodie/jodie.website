## Productivity and Happiness are the Same

The idea of work-life balance is harmful.

### Work isn't Hard

It's tragic that we accept work is unfun. We shouldn't accept that we're forced to do something that sucks. This framing isn't just bad for you, it makes workers as a group accept terrible working conditions. This shouldn't be considered “normal”.

### Fun isn't Easy

Most people's idea of fun is spending money and engaging in vice. People see fun as synonymous with being counter-productive. You're expected to continuously destroy what you build in an endless cycle of consumption that never leads to your life improving.

### Work-Life Maximisation

Productivity and happiness aren't opposing forces that need to be balanced. They aren't at odds with each other. Work can be fun, and leisure can be productive. An activity can be more fun _and_ more productive. What you want isn't "balance". You want work-life _maximisation_.
